Term: The Summer Storm
Definition: A metaphor that represents a sequence of events where after a period of stagnation and unbearable conditions, a sudden change brings excitement, fear, and a sense of release. 

Term: Reversal 
Definition: A situation where normal operations or circumstances are turned or flipped, often resulting in unforeseen outcomes. The context determines its positive or negative impact.

Term: Mutual consent 
Definition: A condition where two or more parties agree upon the same thing in the same sense. In the context of the book, if two people come together with mutual consent, it is not considered seduction as both parties have willingly agreed to it.

Term: Seduction
Definition: The act of enticing or attracting someone, often in a sexual context. If both parties are willingly involved, according to the context of the book, it is not considered seduction.
