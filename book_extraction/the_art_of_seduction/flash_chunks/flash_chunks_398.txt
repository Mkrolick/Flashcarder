Term: Seductive Environment 
Definition: Spaces with a theatrical, heightened quality—like opulence, glittering surfaces, a playful spirit—which create a buoyant, childlike feeling that makes it hard for the victim to think straight. 

Term: Seductive Time
Definition: The creation of an altered sense of time—memorable, dizzying moments that stand out, creating a mood of festival and play.

Term: Transformation in Seduction
Definition: The process in which the victims slowly come to feel an inner change. Under the seducer's influence, they lower their defenses, feeling free to act differently and be a different person.

Term: Real World Vs Seduction.
Definition: In seduction, the victims are made to feel that being with the seducer offers them a different experience than being in the real world.
