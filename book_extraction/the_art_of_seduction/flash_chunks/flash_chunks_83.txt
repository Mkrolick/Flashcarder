Term: The Eighteen Types
Definition: A concept in seduction, where people potentially liable to be seduced are classified according to what they feel they are lacking in life like adventure, attention, romance, a stimulating experience, etc.

Term: Victim
Definition: In the context of seduction, a person who feels they are missing something in life. Identifying this missing component forms the basis for successful seduction.

Term: Reality Behind Appearance
Definition: A concept in seduction where a person's true desires or feelings are often different from their outward behavior or stated preferences. 

Term: Seduction
Definition: A process where one person deliberately entices or attracts another, often by giving them something they feel they lack in life.

Term: Own Type
Definition: In context of seduction, the similar type of individual or potential victim as oneself. It is suggested to avoid trying to seduce this type.
