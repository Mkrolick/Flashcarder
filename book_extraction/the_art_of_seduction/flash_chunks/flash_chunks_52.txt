Term: Harsh Daily Life 
Definition: The daily struggles and challenges that most individuals constantly seek an escape from, often through fantasies and dreams.

Term: Stars 
Definition: Individuals who stand out from others through a distinctive and appealing style, invoking desire in others to watch them.

Term: Vague and Ethereal 
Definition: Descriptive for Stars who, while shining brightly, maintain their distance, allowing others to imagine more than what is actually present. 

Term: Dreamlike Quality 
Definition: A characteristic of Stars, working on people's unconscious, often leading them into imitation without them realizing it. 

Term: Object of Fascination 
Definition: Becoming an entity of interest and admiration by projecting a glittering yet elusive presence similar to a star.
