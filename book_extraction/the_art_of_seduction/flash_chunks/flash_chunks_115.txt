Term: Choose the Right Victim
Definition: This concept emphasizes the importance of selecting a person who is open to your advances and can provide the interest and pursuit you require. Choosing the wrong person could lead to wasted effort and no significant gain.

Term: Reversal
Definition: In this context, Reversal refers to the idea that there is no benefit in attempting to seduce a person who is not receptive to your attempts or cannot provide the excitement and engagement you desire. This suggests there's no potential gain or advantage in reversing or backtracking one's strategy once it is set.
