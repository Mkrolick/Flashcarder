Term: Appear to Be an Object of Desire - Create Triangles
Definition: This involves creating an aura of desirability. It is the process of drawing people closer by making them jealous or envious of the people who are already attracted to you. The idea is to create the illusion of being desired by many which raises your value and makes people want to win your attention and approval.

Term: Manufacture the illusion of popularity
Definition: This involves surrounding yourself with people who appear to be admirers or suitors, raising your attractiveness and apparent desirability. This technique includes involving friends, former lovers, or current admirers to represent your popularity.

Term: Build a reputation that precedes you
Definition: Develop a reputation that makes you attractive to others before they meet you. If many people have fallen for your charms, it indicates that you are desirable and this perception will make others more attracted to you.
