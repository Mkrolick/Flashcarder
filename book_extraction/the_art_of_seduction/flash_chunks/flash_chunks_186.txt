Term: Suspense
Definition: The act of leading someone along by establishing uncertainty and unpredictability about what comes next, usually employed as a tool to maintain control and power. 

Term: Calculated Surprise
Definition: A strategic, unexpected move aimed at instilling a sense of excitement and curiosity in people, thus, drawing them closer towards you.

Term: Mystery
Definition: A key element in the seduction process that provokes people's interest and lures them further into your sphere of influence.
  
Term: Spontaneity
Definition: A strategic, non-predictable behaviour that adds to the thrill of not being able to foresee what comes next, effectively keeping the individual in control. 

Term: Sudden Change of Direction
Definition: A tactic used to provide a thrill to the "victim," maintaining a sense of mystery and control by being one step ahead.
