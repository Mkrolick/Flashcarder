Term: Upraised Middle Finger
Definition: A gesture considered offensive and signifying a negative interaction. Its interpretation can vary across different cultures.

Term: Scrunced Nose
Definition: A type of foe signal that can make anyone observing it less likely to see the person in a positive light.

Term: Clothing and Accessories
Definition: Elements that can act as friend or foe signals depending on their interpretation by the observers. The same item can have different meanings in different contexts or cultures.

Term: Perception Management 
Definition: The process of controlling or influencing how one is perceived by others. The details, such as the quality of one's accessories or clothing, can expose the true nature of a person. 

Term: Foe Signals
Definition: Nonverbal communications or elements that give negative impressions, making others less likely to engage or perceive the person positively.
