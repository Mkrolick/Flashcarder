Term: Rapport
Definition: A close and harmonious relationship in which the individuals or groups understand each other's feelings or ideas and communicate well.

Term: Nonverbal Behavioral Cues 
Definition: These are forms of communication that do not use words, including gestures, facial expressions, body posture, eye contact, physical touch, and others, used to express or convey information.

Term: Feelings Description Using Nonverbal Cues 
Definition: The process of explaining emotions or sentiments of individuals towards each other, based on observation of their nonverbal cues like body language and facial expressions. 

Term: Identifying Friend Signal 
Definition: The process of recognizing signs of friendship not evident in other contexts to determine the relation or rapport between two individuals. These could be unique gestures, specific types of eye contact, or other forms of nonverbal communication.
