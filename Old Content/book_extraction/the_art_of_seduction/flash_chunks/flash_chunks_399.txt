Term: Ardent Rake
Definition: The term refers to a charming and impudent individual who has a strong, often irresistible, novelty about him. He is depicted as highly persuasive and sometimes seen as a seducer or heartbreaker.

Term: Louis XIV
Definition: Louis XIV, also known as Louis the Great (Louis le Grand) or the Sun King (le Roi-Soleil), was King of France from 14 May 1643 until his death in 1715. His reign of 72 years and 110 days is the longest recorded of any monarch of a sovereign country in history.

Term: Duke de Richelieu
Definition: A title associated with Fronsac, the devilishly handsome and impudent individual, known for his irresistible charm and bold behavior. The title's history connects to the infamous Cardinal Richelieu.

Term: Mademoiselle de Valois
Definition: Daughter of Duke d'Orleans, portrayed as a young woman whose heart was smitten by the Duke de Richelieu. Protected by a chaperone due to her father's protective nature, she ultimately finds herself the subject of Richelieu's pursuits.

Term: Bastille
Definition: A fortress in Paris, known formally as the Bastille Saint-Antoine. It represented royal authority in the center of Paris. The prison contained just seven inmates at the time of its storming, but was seen by the revolutionaries as a symbol of the monarchy's dictatorial rule.

Term: Don Juan 
Definition: A legendary, fictional character famous for his many sexual conquests. He is presented here in a dialog with a woman named Tisbea, after being washed ashore from the sea, symbolizing a transition from one perilous situation to another.

Term: Tisbea
Definition: A woman who finds Don Juan washed up on a beach and engages in a flirtatious conversation with him. She warns him about the potential dangers of his fiery personality but also expresses a hopeful expectation of his promising flame.
