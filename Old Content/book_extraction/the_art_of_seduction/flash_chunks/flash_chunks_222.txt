Term: Power of Attraction 
Definition: The ability to draw people in and hold them in our thrall. This latent potential exists within us and can be maximized by developing certain character traits that naturally excite people.

Term: Successful Seductions 
Definition: They begin with your character, your ability to radiate some quality that attracts people and stirs their emotions in a way that is beyond their control. This ability aids in further manipulations without arousing suspicion. 

Term: Nine Seducer Types 
Definition: The distinct character traits which create a seductive pull. These types are Sirens, Rakes, Ideal Lovers, Dandies, Naturals, Coquettes, Charmers, Charismatics, and Stars.

Term: Sirens 
Definition: Seducers who have an abundance of sexual energy and know how to use it.

Term: Rakes 
Definition: Seducers that insatiably adore the opposite sex, and their desire is infectious.

Term: Ideal Lovers 
Definition: Seducers that have an aesthetic sensibility that they apply to romance.

Term: Dandies 
Definition: Seducers who like to play with their image, creating a striking and androgynous allure.

Term: Naturals 
Definition: Seducers that are spontaneous and open.

Term: Coquettes 
Definition: Seducers that are self-sufficient, with a fascinating cool at their core.

Term: Anti-Seducer
