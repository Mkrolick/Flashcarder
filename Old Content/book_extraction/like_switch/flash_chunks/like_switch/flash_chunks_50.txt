Term: Endgame 
Definition: The final outcome or resolution of a situation or sequence of events.

Term: Choice
Definition: The opportunity to select from two or more possibilities when making a decision.

Term: Alternate Response Question
Definition: A technique used to provide an illusion of control, while the person asking the question actually limits the available choices.

Term: Food For Thought
Definition: Something worthy of careful consideration.

Term: Status Elevation
Definition: A verbal technique used to make people feel better about themselves and see you as a friend.

Term: Janitor
Definition: A person employed to clean and maintain a building.

Term: Graffiti 
Definition: Writing or drawings scribbled, scratched, or sprayed illicitly on a wall or other surface in a public place.
